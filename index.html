Ad astra abyssosque!
I'm a final-year undergraduate student from ACM Honor Class, Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU). I am currently a visiting student at Carnegie Mellon University (CMU) at LeCAR Lab, adviced by Prof. Guanya Shi. Since 2023, I have been part of the APEX Data & Knowledge Management Lab at SJTU, advised by Prof. Weinan Zhang and Prof. Yong Yu. My research focus lies in the intersection of learning (ML, RL, IL) and control on robotics, aiming to excavate the potential of versatility, agility and intelligence of legged robots like humanoids and quadrupeds.
