Yichao Zhong

I am a PhD student in Robotics at HKU, advised by Prof. Hongyang Li and Prof. Jia Pan. During my undergraduate years at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, I was fortunate to work with Prof. Weinan Zhang at SJTU and Prof. Guanya Shi at CMU, and honored to intern at Shanghai AI Lab and Horizon Robotics.

My research seeks to unlock the adaptivity and agility of humanoids — spanning adaptive and agile and robust locomotion, interaction and motion skills. Find more in my Curriculum Vitae.
